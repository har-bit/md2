CHOOSING A GOAT BREEDING

With a diverse selection of goat breeds such as Sardi, Jamunapari, Boer, Suffolk, Barbari, Welsh Mountain Sheep, Beetal, Icelandic Sheep, Sirohi, Dorper, Dorset Horn, and Kalahari available, goat farmers have a range of options to consider based on their specific production goals and environmental conditions. Each breed possesses unique characteristics in terms of milk production, meat quality, adaptability to various climates, and resistance to diseases. For instance, Jamunapari and Beetal goats are renowned for their high milk production, while Boer goats are prized for their meat quality. Suffolk and Dorset Horns are popular for meat production, while Sirohi and Kalahari goats are known for their hardiness in arid climates.
